Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the land free from government acquisition?
Yes. All our properties are thoroughly vetted and verified to be free from government acquisition, encumbrances, and disputes. We conduct comprehensive due diligence before listing any estate.
Can I inspect the property before paying?
Absolutely. We encourage all prospective buyers to visit and inspect any property of interest. Contact us to schedule a guided physical inspection at your convenience.
What happens after I complete payment?
Upon full payment, we immediately process your Deed of Conveyance, Registered Survey Plan, and Irrevocable Power of Attorney. You'll receive all original documents, and the land becomes legally yours.
Are there any hidden charges?
No. Transparency is one of our core values. The price you see is the price you pay. Documentation processing, survey plans, and legal fees are all included. There are zero hidden charges.
Can I resell my land later?
Yes. Once you own the land with complete documentation, you are free to resell at any time. Many of our clients have realized significant returns by holding and selling as values appreciate.
