How It All Began

Built on Trust, Driven by Purpose

Jecyani Properties was founded in 2022 with a singular, powerful vision: to democratize land ownership in Nigeria through transparency, integrity, and unwavering client dedication.

What began as a passionate response to the complexities and opacities in the Nigerian real estate market has blossomed into a distinguished enterprise serving over six cities, with ten successfully completed estates and counting.

Our founder, Jane Nwakanma, recognized that for too many Nigerians, the dream of owning land was marred by bureaucratic hurdles, hidden costs, and a profound lack of trust. She built Jecyani Properties to be the antidote — a firm where every transaction is crystal clear, every document is guaranteed, and every client is treated as family.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerians with trusted real estate opportunities, transparent guidance, and flexible investment solutions that create lasting generational wealth through property ownership.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted real estate partner — the undisputed first choice for anyone seeking to build wealth through secure, verified, and profitable land investments.

Meet Our Founder

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Jane Nwakanma

Visionary Entrepreneur & Real Estate Strategist

Jane Nwakanma is the driving force behind Jecyani Properties. With a deep understanding of the Nigerian real estate landscape and an unwavering commitment to ethical business practices, she has positioned the company as a beacon of trust in an industry often characterized by uncertainty.

Her hands-on approach ensures that every client relationship is personal. She believes that real estate is not merely about transactions — it is about building legacies, securing futures, and empowering families with the pride of land ownership.

"At Jecyani Properties, we believe real estate should be built on trust, clarity, and long-term value. I personally ensure every client is treated like family — because your investment is the foundation of your generational wealth."

Under her leadership, Jecyani Properties has grown from a startup to a multi-city enterprise, earning the confidence of investors across Aba, Umuahia, Abuja, Asaba, Enugu, and Port Harcourt.
